# Inequalities (Foundation Basics)

## Explanation
Inequalities use symbols like >, <, ≥, ≤ to show relationships between expressions. Solutions are ranges of values, often shown on number lines.

## Exercises

2. Solve: y - 2 < 8
3. Solve: a + 5 ≥ 12
